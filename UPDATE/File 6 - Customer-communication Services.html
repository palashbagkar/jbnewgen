The India Communication Gap

Why your existing communication stack will underperform in India

Most global startups arrive in India with a communication stack optimised for Western audiences — email-first, app-notification-heavy, and voice-light. India inverts almost every one of these assumptions.

💬

WhatsApp has 98% open rates in India

Email open rates in India average 15–20%. WhatsApp messages are opened within minutes by over 500 million users. For customer updates and partner comms — WhatsApp is the primary channel.

📋

SMS requires DLT registration

India's TRAI regulations require all commercial SMS senders to register on the DLT platform before sending. Non-compliant messages are blocked at the carrier level — silently, with no error.

📞

Voice is dominant in B2B India

Indian enterprise buyers and channel partners close relationships on calls, not email threads. A cloud voice solution is not optional for any India operation managing partners at scale.

🔐

OTP delivery has India-specific challenges

Without India-optimised carrier routing, OTP failure rates can reach 15–20% — enough to materially damage user activation and conversion from day one of your India launch.

OTP Verification & Identity for India

In India, every customer-facing digital transaction that matters requires OTP verification — account creation, payment authorisation, document submission, login security, and identity confirmation. For regulated categories including fintech, healthtech, and e-commerce, this is a regulatory requirement. We implement OTP and two-factor authentication infrastructure optimised for India's carrier environment: multi-carrier routing with intelligent fallback logic, SMS-to-voice fallback for users who don't receive the SMS, and SIM-swap detection integration for categories where fraud risk is high.

Regulatory Compliance

India's communication regulations — what you must know before you send a single message

India has among the world's most structured commercial communication regulatory frameworks. Launching without understanding these will result in messages being blocked, campaigns failing silently, and potential regulatory action. We handle compliance as a built-in part of every engagement.

TRAI DLT Registration Mandatory for all commercial SMS in India. Entity, header, and template registration required before your first send. We manage the full registration process including pre-approval of all message templates — a step most foreign startups skip, causing complete delivery failure at launch.
DPDP Act (2023) India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act governs how customer communication data is stored, processed, and consented to. We build compliant consent frameworks and data handling protocols into every channel setup — so you're protected from day one, not retrofitted after a regulatory query.
Meta WhatsApp Policy WhatsApp Business API usage requires approval and ongoing policy compliance. Message templates must be pre-approved by Meta before use. We handle initial API access approval, template submission and approval, and ongoing policy compliance monitoring as India's WhatsApp business communication rules evolve.
RBI Communication Rules For fintech and payment-related communication, the Reserve Bank of India mandates specific disclosure requirements in customer messaging — including transaction alerts, fraud warnings, and consent confirmation messages. We configure compliant message templates for regulated fintech categories before your first customer transaction.
