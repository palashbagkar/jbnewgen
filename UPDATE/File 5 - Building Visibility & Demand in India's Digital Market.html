Why India's Digital Landscape Is Different

What every global startup gets wrong about digital marketing in India

Your existing digital marketing playbook is almost certainly optimised for Western audiences. Running the same campaigns in India will deliver a fraction of the results — not because the channels don't work, but because the behaviour is fundamentally different.

900M+

India's digital audience

India's internet user base is the world's second largest — and growing faster than any other major market. The opportunity is enormous if you build for it correctly.

YouTube first

Discovery happens on video

For high-consideration B2B and B2C decisions in India, YouTube is the primary research channel. Indian buyers watch product videos and comparisons before they visit a website.

500M+

WhatsApp is a marketing channel

Brands that communicate through WhatsApp — with updates, offers, and content — consistently outperform those that rely on email alone in the Indian market.

Regional language preference

70% of India's internet users are more comfortable in a regional language than English. Regional content significantly outperforms English-only campaigns in Tier 2 and 3 cities.

01

India SEO & Search Presence

Your global website is unlikely to rank for the Indian searches that matter to your buyers — and the keyword landscape is genuinely different from US or UK markets. Indian buyers search more conversationally, use Hindi-English code-switching, and strongly prefer local and city-specific results. We build India-specific SEO from the ground up: keyword research calibrated to Indian search behaviour in your category, localised landing pages for the Indian cities and segments you're targeting, Google Business Profile setup for your India office, technical SEO adapted for India's mobile-first indexing, and a content strategy that answers the questions Indian buyers are actually asking. The output is organic search visibility that compounds over time — the most cost-efficient lead generation channel in India for startups willing to invest early.

Google Ads for India

India is one of the most cost-efficient Google Ads markets in the world — CPCs average 70–85% lower than US rates — but only if your campaigns are structured for how Indians search, what devices they're on, and what conversion actions are realistic at each funnel stage. A US-configured Google Ads account running in India will consistently underperform. We build and manage India-specific campaigns across search, display, and YouTube — with India-native keyword lists, bidding strategies calibrated to Indian CPC benchmarks, ad copy written for Indian buyer motivations, and conversion tracking that accounts for India's longer B2B sales cycles and WhatsApp-first conversion behaviour. Every campaign is reported back to your global team with the metrics that matter: qualified leads, cost-per-acquisition by segment, and pipeline contribution.

WhatsApp & Email Marketing for India

India has over 500 million WhatsApp users, and for a significant portion of Indian businesses and consumers, WhatsApp is their primary digital communication channel. For your India operation, this means WhatsApp is not a nice-to-have marketing channel — it is the channel. We set up WhatsApp Business API campaigns: partner and channel communications, customer onboarding sequences, product update broadcasts, promotional campaigns, and support workflows. All messaging is DLT-registered and compliant with TRAI's commercial communication regulations — a mandatory requirement most foreign startups overlook until they hit a delivery block. Combined with email nurture campaigns built for India's longer B2B sales cycles, this gives you a complete owned-channel communication system from day one.
